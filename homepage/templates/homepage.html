LET'S TALK

A framework for speakers

GConfs is a student association at EPITA organizing highly technical conferences in Paris. It's like a framework for speakers who want to give a talk about any kind of subject directly or indirectly related to computer science.

Tech talks in a nutshell

Everything is organized in such a way, that we take care of all relevant infrastructure, and leave the speaker with only the talk's content to take care of. We believe that the content is what truly matters, which is why many of our members are also speakers and frequently give their own talks.

Have a blast

With a decade of experience, GConfs has worked with the biggest names and shared events with Facebook, Google and EuroBSDcon to name a few. If you think you have something worth sharing, just get in touch with us at contact@gconfs.fr
